IVDY Customers and Users

Learn how IVDY supports various healthcare actors and contributes to optimizing patient care.

1. Patients and End-Consumers

Usage: Patients can use IVDY to monitor their health data in real-time, detect risks early, and receive personalized treatment plans.

  • Receive personalized health recommendations
  • Monitor vital signs like blood pressure, blood sugar, and more
  • Integrate genetic data for precise predictions
  • Data privacy through blockchain technology
  • Easy communication with healthcare providers

2. Healthcare Professionals

A. Cardiologists and Heart Surgeons

Usage: Cardiologists can use IVDY to identify high-risk patients earlier and offer personalized treatment plans. Surgeons benefit from monitoring post-operative recovery and detecting complications early.

  • Early detection of cardiovascular diseases
  • Personalized, data-driven treatment approaches
  • Monitor recovery after surgeries

B. General Practitioners and Internists

Usage: General practitioners and internists can use IVDY for routine screenings and continuous patient monitoring to reduce the likelihood of misdiagnosis and intervene early.

  • Streamlined diagnosis processes
  • Real-time health updates
  • Early intervention

C. Medical Laboratories

Usage: Labs can integrate AI-based diagnostics into their workflows using IVDY, digitalizing lab results and providing more accurate health assessments for doctors and patients.

  • Efficient data transmission and integration
  • Faster and more accurate diagnoses
  • Shorter processing times

3. Hospitals and Clinics

Usage: IVDY can be integrated into hospital and clinic systems to improve patient intake workflows and optimize diagnostic processes.

  • Optimized patient intake and treatment
  • Automated and more accurate diagnostics
  • Improved patient care

4. Health Insurers

Usage: IVDY helps insurers assess health risks more accurately and determine personalized premiums based on AI-powered health risk scores.

  • More accurate risk assessment
  • Reduced healthcare costs
  • Early interventions for cost reduction

5. Researchers & Innovators in Digital Health

Usage: Researchers in cardiology, genetics, and data science use IVDY to collect anonymized health data for studies and train AI models for cardiovascular disease research.

  • Access to secure, anonymized health data
  • Support in developing new health technologies
